01

Principles

Five rules every screen answers to. Use them as a sanity check before shipping.

01
Paper, not pixels
Warm cream over slate. The product is a workspace, not a dashboard.
02
Serifs earn it
Display only. Never inside dense data, forms, or buttons.
03
One yellow
Accent is a stage light, not a paint job. If it's everywhere, it's nowhere.
04
Density on demand
Lists are tight. Editors breathe. Match Shopify density in tables.
05
Soon is honest
Show what's coming. Never disable without a story.

08

Voice & Copy

Short. Specific. Plain. We're a tool, not a hype machine.

Do

Plain & concrete

  • "Send 24 hours after the cart was abandoned"
  • "This flow has been live for 9 days. 1,284 emails delivered."
  • "Contacts can re-enter after a cooldown."
  • "Publish to start sending."
Don't

Vague & salesy

  • "Unlock the power of automation"
  • "Deliver delight to every customer 🚀"
  • "Supercharge your revenue"
  • "Click here to elevate your strategy"
